Study Route

A long-range plan that stays useful whether you move steadily, sprint ahead, or repeat a week.

Weeks 1-16

Build the foundation with certification, aviation basics, aerodynamics, systems, flight instruments, performance, and weight and balance.

Weeks 17-36

Move into weather, aviation services, airports, airspace, communications, navigation, charts, VOR, GPS, and flight planning.

Weeks 37-52

Finish with regulations, aeromedical factors, risk management, cross-country scenarios, remediation, and final written-exam prep.
